Start at Parabiago Station
Head to Parabiago train station. Look for the 🚆 S5 platform.

🟢 GOING — direction to look for on the board
S5 direction TREVIGLIO or PIOLTELLO
Both go through Milan city centre and stop at Repubblica ✅
On the platform screen it may also show "Passante" or "Porta Vittoria" — that's the same train, still correct!

💡 Handy Tips for Sunday
  • Your Trenord STIBM ticket (from the app) covers metro + bus too — no need to buy a separate ATM ticket unless your 90-min validity has run out by the time you reach Repubblica.
  • Check the Trenord app for live S5 timetables on Sunday.
  • Leave Parabiago at least 2 hours before game time — no rush, just relaxed.
  • At Repubblica, follow the big yellow M3 signs — the metro connection is inside the same station.
  • Keep your ticket — inspectors are very active on Milan transit! 🎫
  • For the return trip, shuttles back to Rogoredo depart right after the final whistle.
  • Screenshot this page or save it offline before you go — just in case! 📱
